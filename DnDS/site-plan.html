Overview

Purpose

I want to create a website that can help people who play Dungeons and Dragons. It would have a home page that explains what it is, a page that would generate random dice roles, and a page where you could search for information related to building a character such as races, classes, feats, spells and more. It can be annoying to quickly search up info when you are in a time crunch or playing and this could help solve the issues.

Audience

The intended audience is mostly for myself but could be for anyone who wants to play dungeons and dragons. It will be able to help them create characters and find the information they need. It will provide the basics aso anyone who wants to try out dungeons and dragons could find this very useful. So whether you are brand new or a seasoned player this website could help you, welcome to everyone.

Introdution

Dungeons and Dragons is a fun tabletop role playing game. It is a game where you use your imagination to coauthor a story with friends. It has some set rules to help keep it running and fun for everyone. Here on this website you can find some basic rules to help build characters and the dice you need to play. Have fun and create your own story.
